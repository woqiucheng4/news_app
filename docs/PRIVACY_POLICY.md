# NewsFlow Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-31

NewsFlow ("we", "our", "the app") is an AI-powered news aggregation service. This policy describes what data we collect, why we collect it, and your choices.

## Data We Collect

- **Account information**: email address, display name, and authentication identifiers from email, Google, or Apple sign-in.
- **Subscription preferences**: topics you follow, notification settings, and in-app preferences (theme, language).
- **Usage data**: anonymous analytics events (feed views, search, subscribe actions) when analytics is enabled in Settings.
- **Device tokens**: Firebase Cloud Messaging tokens used to deliver push notifications.
- **Purchase status**: premium subscription state and store verification metadata (no full payment card data).

## How We Use Data

- Personalize your feed and daily briefing based on subscribed topics
- Send push notifications you opt into
- Operate freemium limits and premium features
- Improve reliability and product analytics (aggregated)

## AI Processing

Article summaries and premium deep analysis are generated via third-party AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic). We send article title/summary snippets—not full cached article text—to generate responses.

## Data Storage

Data is stored on servers located outside mainland China, aligned with our GDPR-first approach. We do not sell personal data.

## Your Rights (GDPR)

From the app Settings / account APIs you can:

- Export your account data (`GET /users/me/export`)
- Delete your account (`DELETE /users/me`)

Contact: privacy@newsflow.app (replace with your production contact before store submission).

## Third-Party Services

- Supabase Auth
- Firebase Cloud Messaging & Analytics
- OpenAI / Anthropic APIs
- Apple App Store / Google Play billing (when enabled)

## Children

NewsFlow is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in the EU).

## Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected in the app or store listing.
